Charmaine Sheh Sze-Man Performer / ممثل

Charmaine Sheh Sze-Man is a celebrated actress from Hong Kong, born on May 28, 1975. She gained recognition after becoming the second runner-up in the 1997 Miss Hong Kong Pageant. Following this, she signed with TVB, Hong Kong's leading television network, and began her acting career. Charmaine is best known for her roles in popular TV dramas such as Return of the Cuckoo (2000), War and Beauty (2004), Beyond the Realm of Conscience (2009), and Story of Yanxi Palace (2018). Her performances have earned her multiple awards, including the TVB Anniversary Award for Best Actress. Charmaine also holds a diploma in hotel management from the International Hotel Management Institute in Switzerland, showcasing her diverse talents. Over the years, she has expanded her career to mainland China, gaining international fame for her work in period dramas and modern series alike.
