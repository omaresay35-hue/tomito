Park Jeong-min Performer | ممثل

Park Jeong-min (born March 24, 1987) is a South Korean actor. He is best known for starring in the films Bleak Night (2011), Keys to the Heart (2018), Deliver Us from Evil (2020), and Newtopia (2025). He is also the writer of best-selling book A Useful Person.
