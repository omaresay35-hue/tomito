TeeTee Wanpichit Nimitphakphum is a Thai actor under DOMUNDI TV. He's currently studying for a bachelor's degree at the University of Thai Chamber of Commerce's Business School, majoring in Digital Marketing. TeeTee started in the entertainment industry in 2022 when he was a contestant on the 'To Be Number One Idol 12'. He made his acting debut in 2024 with a guest role in the series "Your Sky".
