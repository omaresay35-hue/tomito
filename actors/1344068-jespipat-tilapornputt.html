Jespipat Tilapornputt Performer / ممثل

Jes Jespipat Tilapornputt, originally nicknamed Por, is a Thai actor managed by The One Enterprise and Be On Cloud. He's the nephew of famed jazz saxophonist Metawat Sapsanyakorn. After graduating from Bangkok Christian College, he went on to study at Kasetsart Business School, obtaining a master's in applied finance with a GPA of 3.91. Jes made his first foray into the entertainment industry in 2009 when he won the Pop Da'vance competition and was persuaded by director Poj Arnon to act in the films "Oh My Ghosts!" and "Sassy Player". Jes landed his first role as a lakorn leading man in "Cheewit Puer Kah Huajai Puer Tur". Jes's family owns Pornpat Market in Rangsit.
