Park Ji-hoon Performer | ممثل

Park Ji-hoon (Hangul: 박지훈; born May 29, 1999) is a South Korean singer and actor under YY Entertainment. He first rose to fame as a member of the K-pop project boy group Wanna One. Following the group’s disbandment in 2019, Ji-hoon pursued a solo music career and expanded into acting, building on his early experience as a child actor.
