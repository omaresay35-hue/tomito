Leyla Smyrna Cabas Performer | ممثل

Leyla Smyrna Cabas (born 2010, Istanbul) is a Turkish film actress. She was born in Istanbul, Turkey, in 2010. She speaks Turkish, English, and German fluently. She appeared in an independent film shot in Turkey, written and directed by Ayçıl Yeltan. Most recently, she starred in the film “Yellow Letters,” directed by İlker Çatak and filmed in Germany.
