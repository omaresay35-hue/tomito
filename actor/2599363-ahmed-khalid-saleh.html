Ahmed Khalid Saleh Performer | ممثل

An Egyptian actor, son of late actor Khaled Saleh. He graduated from the American University, Theater Department. He participated in theatrical shows on the American University stage, including Police and Thieves, then made his TV debut in the series 30 Days.
