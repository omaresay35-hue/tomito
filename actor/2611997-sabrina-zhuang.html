Sabrina Zhuang Performer | ممثل

Sabrina Zhuang or Zhuang Dafei is a Chinese actress and singer born on February 28, 2001 in Shenyang City, Liaoning Province. She made her acting debut in the 2012 short film, Old Man's Wish. Her main acting works include Miss Puff, Mountains and Ocean, Closer to You, I Don't Want To Be Friends With You, Love Script, Rock It, Mom, and You Are Desire.
