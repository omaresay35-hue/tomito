Kouki Osuzu Performer | ممثل

Kouki Osuzu (大鈴 功起, Ōsuzu Kōki, born October 5) is a Japanese voice actor (seiyuu) from Tokyo who is affiliated with Vims. His hobbies and special skills include rakugo, games, and basketball. He graduated from the Japan Narration Actor Institute.
