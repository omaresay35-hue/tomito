Geraldine Indira Viswanathan (/ˌvɪswəˈnɑːθən/VISS-wə-NAH-thən; born 20 June 1995) is an Australian actress, comedian, writer and producer. She gained attention in the role of Kayla in the 2018 film Blockers, for which Refinery29 referred to her as "the film's breakout star". Viswanathan starred in the 2019 coming-of-age film Hala and Bad Education.
