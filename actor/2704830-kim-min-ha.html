Kim Min-ha Performer | ممثل

Kim Min-ha (born September 1, 1995), is a South Korean actress. She debuted in 2016 through the web drama "Two Women's Season 2", followed by "School 2017", "Gun Law Men and Women", and "It's Okay to be Insignificant". She gained international recognition for her role as the young adult Sunja in Apple TV+ series Pachinko in 2022.
