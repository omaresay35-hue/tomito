Lola Tung Performer | ممثل

Lola Tung is an actress, known for The Summer I Turned Pretty (2022). A New York native, Lola graduated from Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts and spent a year at Carnegie Mellon University in The School of Drama Acting and Music Theater. Shortly after landing the leading role in The Summer I Turned Pretty, she signed with the American talent agency, Creative Artists Agency.
