Ren Suxi Performer | ممثل

Ren Suxi graduated from the Central Academy of Drama, majoring in directing. She is a powerful actor who has risen rapidly on the stage of drama since 2010. Her performances are relaxed, natural, flexible and explosive. In 2011, Ren played multiple roles in the stage drama "Three People Can't Do It" by Li Guoxiu. In 2012, she starred in "Mr. Donkey" by directors Zhou Shen and Liu Lu.
