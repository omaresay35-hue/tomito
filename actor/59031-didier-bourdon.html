Didier Bourdon Performer | ممثل

Didier Bourdon (born 23 January 1959) is a French actor, screenwriter, and film director. He first reached stardom in France when he created the comedic trio Les Inconnus with Bernard Campan and Pascal Légitimus, which was very popular throughout the 1990s.
