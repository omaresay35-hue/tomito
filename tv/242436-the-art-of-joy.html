L'arte della gioia The Art of Joy

Born on the 1st of January 1900 in a poor family in rural Sicily, Modesta pursues happiness since her childhood without falling victim to the constraints of society. Later, she arrives at the villa belonging to the Princess of Brandiforti, where she makes herself indispensable.
