Ami Tomite Performer | ممثل

Tomite Ami was an AKB48 Kenkyuusei. She joined AKB48 as Kenkyuusei on 2 September 2009 and graduated from AKB48 Kenkyuusei (3rd Kenkyuusei Selection) on 4 December 2009. She has released 9 gravure DVDs between 2010 and 2014 and is now frequently cast in movies, such as "Riaru Onigokko" with Shinoda Mariko or "Eiga Minna! Esupa Dayo!" with Itano Tomomi.
