Taito Ban Performer | ممثل

Taito Ban (坂 泰斗, Ban Taito, December 18, 1992) is a Japanese voice actor born in Fukuoka Prefecture who is affiliated with Office Osawa. Graduated from Pro-Fit Voice Actor Training Center, he was represented by Pro-Fit since April 2017 until he transferred to Office Osawa in January 2022 as the agency would end its talent management business in March 2022.
