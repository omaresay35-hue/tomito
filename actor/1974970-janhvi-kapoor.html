Janhvi Kapoor Performer | ممثل

Janhvi Kapoor (born 6 March 1997) is an Indian actress who has worked in Hindi and Telugu films. Born to film actress Sridevi and producer Boney Kapoor, she made her acting debut in 2018 with the romantic drama Dhadak, which was a commercial success. In 2024, she had a brief role in the Telugu action drama Devara: Part 1.
