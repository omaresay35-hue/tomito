Kristy Wordsworth Performer | ممثل

Kristy Wordsworth was born & bred in Sydney, Australia. Having loved performing from a young age, Kristy studied Drama throughout her school years before becoming a self-confessed travel addict and joining the airlines as a Flight Attendant in 2010. In between traveling the world, she appeared in numerous TVC's, print & corporate video's as well as extra/featured extra work, and in 2017 decided to continue studying her passion in the performing arts.
