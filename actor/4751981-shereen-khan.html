Shereen Khan Performer | ممثل

Shereen Khan is an East Indian-American SAG-AFTRA actress based in Los Angeles, CA. Her work includes roles on S.W.A.T. Season 6, The Chosen Season 4, comedy series The Promised Land, and the award-winning kiddie rock musical Gwendolyn and the Good Gang. Shereen's ongoing training includes Stella Adler Academy, Beverly Hills Playhouse, and she graduated as a theatre major from top-ranking art school, LACHSA.
