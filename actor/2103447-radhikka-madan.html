Radhikka Madan Performer | ممثل

Radhika Madan is an Indian television and film actress. Before her debut, Madan used to work as a dance instructor in New Delhi. Madan started her career by playing the role of Ishani in Ekta Kapoor's Meri Aashiqui Tum Se Hi on Colors TV. She won the 'Best Debut' Award at Zee Gold Awards 2015. She was also seen in Colors TV's dance reality show Jhalak Reloaded. Madan is debuting in Bollywood with Vishal Bhardwaj's Pataakha.
