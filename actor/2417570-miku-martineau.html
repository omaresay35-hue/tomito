Miku Martineau (born Miku Patricia Martineau, 13 September 2004) is a Canadian actress, voiceover artist, and singer. She gained prominence for her role as Ani in the film Kate (2021) and has since appeared in various film and television projects, including Honor Society (2022), Star Trek: Section 31 (2025), and the Netflix series Bet (2025).
