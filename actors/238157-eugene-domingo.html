Eugene Domingo Performer / ممثل

Eugene Domingo (born Eugene Roxas Domingo on 23 July 1971) is a Filipino actress and television personality. She is best known for her roles in the film franchises Ang Tanging Ina (2003-2011), Kimmy Dora (2009-2012), and Ang Babae sa Septic Tank (2011-2019), as well as for the films Here Comes The Bride (2010), Barber's Tales (2014), Ten Little Mistresses (2023), And the Breadwinner Is... (2024), and Kontrabida Academy (2025), among others.
