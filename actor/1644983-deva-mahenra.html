Deva Mahenra Performer | ممثل

Deva Mahenra (born April 19, 1990) is an Indonesian actor, presenter, singer, and flight attendant. Deva began his career as a finalist in the Top 40 of Indonesian Idol Season 4 in 2007. He made his acting debut in 2012 through the television series Saranghae I Love You. His big-screen debut came in 2013 when he starred in the film 'Slank Nggak Ada Matinya' (2013). Deva has hosted Entertainment News and The Remix on NET, as well as worked as a DJ at several events.
