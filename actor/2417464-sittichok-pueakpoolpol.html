Sittichok Pueakpoolpol Performer | ممثل

Tommy Sittichok Pueakpoolpol is a Thai actor and singer under DOMUNDI TV and DMD Music. He graduated with a bachelor's degree from the Ramkhamhaeng University. He made his acting debut in 2015 with a main role in the series "LOVE SMART". In July 2020, he officially debuted as a solo artist with the release of his first single "Tell Now?". Tommy was also a member of the project boy group BOYFRIENDS.
