Céleste Brunnquell Performer | ممثل

Céleste Brunnquell made her film debut in 2019 in Sarah Suco’s Les Éblouis (The Dazzled), for which she was nominated for a César for Best New Actress and won the Best Actress prize at the Sarlat Festival. She then starred alongside Dominique Blanc, Laure Calamy, Doria Tillier and Jacques Weber in L’Origine du mal (The Origin of Evil) by Sébastien Marnier.
