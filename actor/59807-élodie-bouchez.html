Élodie Bouchez Performer | ممثل

Élodie Bouchez-Bangalter (born 5 April 1973) is a French actress. She is best-known in the United States for her role as Renée Rienne on the fifth and final season of the television show Alias. She is also well-known for playing Maïté Alvarez in the movie Wild Reeds.
