Jean-Carl Boucher Performer | ممثل

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia. Jean-Carl Boucher is a young Québécois actor, best known for his role as Diego Molina on the show Tactik shown on Télé-Québec. He has also starred in Un été sans point ni coup sûr and 1981. He is good friends with actor Alexandre Bacon who often provides voiceovers.
