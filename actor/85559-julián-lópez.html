Julián López Performer | ممثل

Julián López was born on November 10, 1978 in El Provencio, Cuenca, Castilla-La Mancha, Spain as Julián López González. He is an actor and writer, known for Noche Hache (2005),Pagafantas (2009) and Museo Coconut (2010).
