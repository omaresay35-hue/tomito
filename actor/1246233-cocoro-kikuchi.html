Cocoro Kikuchi Performer | ممثل

Cocoro Kikuchi (菊池 こころ, Kikuchi Cocoro, November 9, 1982) is a Japanese voice actress born in Tokyo. She is affiliated with Kenyu Office, previously worked for Toei Academy until March, 2011. Her old stage name is Kokoro Kikuchi (菊池 心, Kikuchi Kokoro).
