Li Xueqin Performer | ممثل

Li Xue Qin, born in Tieling City, Liaoning Province in 1995, is an internet celebrity and talk show actor in Mainland China. In 2019, Li Xueqin received everyone's attention for recording a video of Wu Yifan, Guo Ailun, Li Yanhong, and Yang Ming who were replied by them. Netizens called it "Star Chasing Koi". In 2020, the talk show competition program "Talk Show Conference Season 3" participated in the recording was broadcast on Tencent Video, and finally won the fifth place in the finals.
