Aparna Balamurali Performer | ممثل

Aparna Balamurali is an Indian actress and playback singer who works mostly in Malayalam and Tamil cinema. She is known for her roles in Maheshinte Prathikaaram, Sunday Holiday and Soorarai Pottru. She won the National Film Award for the Best Actress for playing Bommi in Sudha Kongara’s hugely successful film Soorarai Pottru.
