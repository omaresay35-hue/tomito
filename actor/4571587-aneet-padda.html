Aneet Padda Performer | ممثل

Aneet Padda (born 13 October 2002) is an Indian actress who works in Hindi film and series. She began her career with a brief role in the drama Salaam Venky (2022) and had her first lead role in the Amazon Prime Video series Big Girls Don't Cry (2024). Padda had her breakthrough with Mohit Suri's romantic drama Saiyaara (2025), for which she earned wider recognition.
