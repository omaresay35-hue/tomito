Sae Hiratsuka Performer | ممثل

Sae Hiratsuka (平塚 紗依, Hiratsuka Sae, October 12, 2004) is a Japanese voice actress from Tokyo who is affiliated with Office Osawa. In 2022, she made her debut as Meico Kaneo in the TV anime Me & Roboco. In 2024, she will star as one of the double leads as Mitsuki Hoshikawa in Himitsu no AiPri.
