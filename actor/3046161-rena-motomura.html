Rena Motomura Performer | ممثل

Rena Motomura (本村 玲奈, Motomura Rena, born October 10) is a Japanese voice actress (seiyu) from Osaka Prefecture who is affiliated with Across Entertainment. Her hobbies include drawing, scriptwriting, and listening to music, and her special skills include classical ballet (5 years) and kyokushin karate (3 years).
