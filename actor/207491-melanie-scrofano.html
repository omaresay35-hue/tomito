Melanie Scrofano Performer | ممثل

Melanie Neige Scrofano is a Canadian actress. From 2016 to 2021, Scrofano starred as the title character on the Syfy modern Western drama Wynonna Earp. In 2019, she played Emilie in the comedy horror film Ready or Not.
