Marian Rivera Performer | ممثل

Marian Rivera Gracia Dantes (born 12 August 1984) is a Filipino actress and television personality. She is best known for her roles in the films My Best Friend's Girlfriend (2008), You To Me Are Everything (2010), Rewind (2023), and Balota (2024). She won Best Actress at the 73rd Filipino Academy of Movie Arts and Sciences (FAMAS) Awards and the 20th Cinemalaya Awards for Balota.
