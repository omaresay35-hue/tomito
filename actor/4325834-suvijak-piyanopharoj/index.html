Suvijak Piyanopharoj Performer | ممثل

Keen Suvijak Piyanopharoj is an actor and singer under GMMTV. After graduating from Assumption College, he now is studying engineering at Chulalongkorn University. He is known for competing on GMMTV's reality survival show "Project Alpha". He made his acting debut in 2023 with a supporting role in the series "My Tempo". In November 2025, he was revealed as a member of the boy group CLO'VER under Riser Music.
