Francesca Reale Performer | ممثل

Francesca Reale is an American actress who has appeared in the Netflix series Haters Back Off and Stranger Things. Reale is known for her role as Emily in Haters Back Off, where she played Miranda Sing's sister. In Stranger Things season three, she played Heather, a popular lifeguard at the community pool who becomes central to a terrifying sequence. Reale's film debut was in the independent drama Yes, God, Yes, which premiered at the 2019 SXSW Film Festival.
