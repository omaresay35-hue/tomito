Hwang Bo-reum-byeol Performer | ممثل

Hwang Bo Reum Byeol is a South Korean actress under Mystic Story. She is the winner of 2019 Miss Chunhyang beauty pageant. She drew attention through a number of pictorials including 'UNIV tomorrow' and 'ARENA' in 2019. She made her acting debut in the 2020 web series based on a webtoon "The World of My 17" and the short movie "Departure, Sun".
