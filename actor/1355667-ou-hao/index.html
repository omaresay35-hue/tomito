Ou Hao Performer | ممثل

Ou Hao (欧豪, born in Pingtan County, Fuzhou City, Fujian Province) is a Chinese pop singer and film actor, also known mononymously as OHO. In 2014, he appeared in his first TV series, "Singing Wars". In 2015, he released his first personal EP album, "On His Own", winning awards as a pop singer. In 2019, he starred in the drama film "Heroes of Fire" and the action crime film "Go Risky" and was nominated for the Best Supporting Actor Award at the 11th Macau International Film Festival.
