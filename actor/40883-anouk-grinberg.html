Anouk Grinberg Performer | ممثل

Anouk Grinberg, daughter of writer Michel Vinaver. First a theater actress, she has performed in both private and public theaters, under the direction, among others, of Jean-Louis Martinelli, Patrice Chéreau, Didier Bezace, Jacques Lassalle, Alain Françon. In cinema or for television, she has toured in particular with Bertrand Blier, Jacques Audiard, Gilles Bourdos, Nina Companeez. She also performs in public readings. For several years, she has also exhibited her drawings and paintings.
