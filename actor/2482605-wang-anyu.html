Wang Anyu Performer | ممثل

Wang Anyu (王安宇) is a Chinese actor born on February 3, 1998, in Yuyao, Zhejiang, China. He graduated from the Communication University of China, majoring in Broadcasting & Hosting. He made his acting debut in 2019 with the youth drama Our Shiny Days. Since then, he has starred in several popular dramas, including Dreaming Back to the Qing Dynasty (2019), Twenty Your Life On (2020), The Heiress (2020), To Fly With You (2021), Falling Into You (2022), and The Last Immortal (2023).
