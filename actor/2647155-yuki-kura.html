Yuki Kura Performer | ممثل

Yuki Kura (倉 悠貴) is a Japanese actor and model born in Osaka on December 19, 1999. In 2018, he was scouted by Sony Music Artists who saw a photo of him published in the men's fashion magazine MEN'S NON-NO and subsequently entered the entertainment world. He made his debut as an actor in 2019 with his role in Trace (トレース～科捜研の男～).
