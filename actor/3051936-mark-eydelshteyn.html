Mark Alexandrovich Eydelshteyn (born February 18, 2002) is a Russian actor. He gained fame after playing the leading role in the drama The Land of Sasha (2022). He is most prominently recognized for his role as Ivan "Vanya" Zakharov in Anora (2024), directed by Sean Baker.
