Yuu Asakawa Performer | ممثل

Yuu Asakawa (born 20.03.1975), is a popular Japanese voice actress from Tokyo. She is also a singer, both solo and with the Hinata Girls. She also provides the voice samples for the third Vocaloid, Megurine Luka. Asakawa was affiliated with Arts Vision and is currently a freelance voice actress.
