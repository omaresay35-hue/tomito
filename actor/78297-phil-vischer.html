Phil Vischer Performer | ممثل

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia. Phillip "Phil" Vischer (born June 16, 1966) is an American voice actor, animator, director, writer and puppeteer known for creating the computer-animated video series VeggieTales with partner and friend Mike Nawrocki.
