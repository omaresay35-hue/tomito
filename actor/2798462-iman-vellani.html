Iman Vellani Performer | ممثل

Iman Vellani (born August 12, 2002) is a Canadian actress and comic book writer. She starred as Kamala Khan in the Marvel Cinematic Universe productions Ms. Marvel (2022) and The Marvels (2023). Vellani has also appeared as Khan in other Disney productions and has co-written two Ms. Marvel limited series.
