Parvathy Thiruvothu Performer | ممثل

Parvathy Thiruvothu is an Indian actress, made her debut in the 2006 Malayalam film "Out of Syllabus". She gained recognition for her work in Malayalam, Tamil, and Kannada cinema - notable films include Bangalore Days (2014) , Ennu Ninte Moideen (2015), and Take Off (2017). Parvathy won the 2015 Kerala State Film Award for Best Actress for her performances in the Malayalam films Ennu Ninte Moideen and Charlie.
