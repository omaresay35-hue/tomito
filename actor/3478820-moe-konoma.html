Moe Konoma Performer | ممثل

Moe Konoma (木間 萌, Konoma Moe, January 14) is a Japanese voice actress born in Niigata Prefecture. She has been affiliated with 81 Produce since April 1, 2019. On August 29, 2018, it was announced that she would be participate in the world's first basketball league for voice actors –Seiyu Jr Basuke 3x3 SJ3.LEAGUE– as a member of the Tweedia team.
