Makoto Furukawa (古川 慎, Furukawa Makoto, born September 29, 1989) is a Japanese voice actor (seiyuu) and singer from Kumamoto Prefecture. He is a member of Toys Factory. He graduated from Fukuoka School of Music. In July 2018, he debuted as a singer under Lantis. On September 1 of the same year, he left Space Craft Entertainment, where he had been affiliated for six years, and joined Toys Factory on December 1 of the same year.
