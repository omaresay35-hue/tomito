Hannu-Pekka Björkman Performer | ممثل

Hannu-Pekka Juhani Björkman (born 11 February 1969) is a Finnish actor. He has won two Jussi Awards for the Best Actor for films For the Living and the Dead (2005) and The Human Part (2018). In 2018, he won a Jussi Award for the Best Supporting Actor for film The Eternal Road (2017).
