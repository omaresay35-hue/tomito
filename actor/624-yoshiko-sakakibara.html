Yoshiko Sakakibara Performer | ممثل

Yoshiko Sakakibara (榊原 良子, Sakakibara Yoshiko, May 31, 1956) is a Japanese actress, voice actress (seiyuu) and narrator. She was born in Senzoku, Taitō City, Tokyo, and grew up in Kashiwa, Chiba Prefecture. She was formerly affiliated with Production Baobab, Tokyo Actor's Life Cooperative (Haikyo) and Combination (until November 1, 2017). She is currently freelance.
