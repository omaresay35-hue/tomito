Napatsakorn Pingmuang Performer | ممثل

Namping Napatsakorn Pingmuang is a Thai actor under DOMUNDI TV. He graduated with a bachelor's degree from Chulalongkorn University's Faculty of Communication Arts, majoring in Journalism, Information and New Media. Namping started in the entertainment industry in 2022 when he played the leading man in Nont Tanont's 'Melt (โต๊ะริม)' music video. He made his acting debut in 2023 with a supporting role in the movie "After Sundown".
