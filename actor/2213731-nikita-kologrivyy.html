Nikita Kologrivyy Performer | ممثل

Nikita Sergeevich Kologrivyy (Russian: Никита Сергеевич Кологривый; born October 16, 1994; Novosibirsk) is a Russian theatre, film, and television actor. He is known for his roles in Igor Zaitsev’s Bender film trilogy, as Lewis in the series Offline, Gera in Two Hills, Yemelya in Wish of the Fairy Fish (2023), and Kashchey in The Boy's Word: Blood on the Asphalt.
