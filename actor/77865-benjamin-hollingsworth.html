From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia. Ben Hollingsworth (born September 7, 1984) is a Canadian actor. He was born in Brockville, Ontario and attended St. Peter's Secondary School in Peterborough. After graduating from high school he enrolled at the National Theatre School of Canada.
