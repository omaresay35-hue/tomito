Antonio Resines Performer | ممثل

Antonio Resines was born on August 7, 1954 in Torrelavega, Cantabria, Spain as Antonio Fernández Resines. He is an actor and producer, known for Los Serrano (2003), A las once en casa (1998) and Celda 211 (2009). He was previously married to Marisol de Mateo.
