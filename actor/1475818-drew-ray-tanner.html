Drew Ray Tanner Performer | ممثل

Drew Ray Tanner (born February 12, 1992) is a Canadian actor from Victoria, British Columbia. He is an actor best known for his role as Fangs Fogarty on Riverdale (2017-2023). Originally, he was only meant to appear in a handful of episodes in season two, but after three years in a recurring role he was promoted to a series regular for seasons five through seven.
