Fedi Nuril Performer | ممثل

Fedrian Nuril is an Indonesian model and actor. He is a part of the Indonesian music band Garasi. Fedi acted in a film of the same name, Garasi (2006), starring with his other band members. The youngest of three, Fedi is mostly known from his role as Fahri in the novel-adapted film Ayat-Ayat Cinta (2008).
