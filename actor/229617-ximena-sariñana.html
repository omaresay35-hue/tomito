Ximena Sariñana Performer | ممثل

​Ximena Sariñana Rivera (born October 29, 1985 in Guadalajara, Mexico) is a Mexican singer and actress for film and television. She debuted in the cinema at the age of 9 in Hasta morir by her father Fernando Sariñana, then played young girl roles in telenovelas before returning to the big screen. She sings in the music group Feliz No cumpleaños ( Happy Birthday) who released the album La Familia Feliz in September 2006.
