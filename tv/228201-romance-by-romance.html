Determined to make this relationship last, Ha Yoon suggests a month-long “break-up grace period” in which she and Tae-ryeon are allowed to spend as much time apart as they want, without calling things off completely. Agreeing to her terms, Tae-ryeon and Ha-yoon draw up a contract in which the terms of their grace period are clearly stated.
