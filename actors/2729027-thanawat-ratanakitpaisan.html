Thanawat Ratanakitpaisan Performer / ممثل

Khaotung Thanawat Ratanakitpaisan is a Thai actor and artist under GMMTV. He studied in the Faculty of Medical Technology at Chiang Mai University where he was the faculty's moon in 2017. He later studied environmental engineering at Kasetsart University and was a finalist in Kasetsart's 2018 Freshy Boy and Girl contest. Khaotung made his acting debut in the 2018 series "'Cause You're My Boy" and had his first starring role in the 2020 series "Tonhon Chonlatee." He has a brand of clothes and bags called A*FLEUR.
