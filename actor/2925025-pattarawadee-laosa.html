Pattarawadee Laosa Performer | ممثل

Tungpang Pattarawadee Laosa is a Thai television actress born in Bangkok, Thailand. She started her career in the entertainment industry in grade three by competing at various events before eventually being cast in the series "BeeTalk" (บีทอล์ค), as well as the sitcom "Nong Mai Rai Borisut" (น้องใหม่ร้ายบริสุทธิ์).
