Shiori Mikami Performer | ممثل

Shiori Mikami is a Japanese voice actress who is affiliated with Aoni Production. She was a junior member of Aoni through 2011. Her best-known roles include lead character Akari Akaza in YuruYuri and Krista Lenz in Attack on Titan. She won Best New Actress at the 6th Seiyu Awards in 2012.
