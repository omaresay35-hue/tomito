Lolly Adefope Performer | ممثل

Ololade "Lolly" Adefope (born September 14, 1990) is a British stand-up comedian and actress, specialising in character comedy. As an actress, she has appeared on Together (2015), Josh (2015), Plebs (2016), Rovers (2016), and Sick Note (2017). She has also participated as a guest on Alan Davies: As Yet Untitled (2016), The Last Leg (2016), Don't Ask Me Ask Britain (2017), and QI (2016). She appeared on the fourth series of the panel show Taskmaster and the 100th-anniversary suffragette special
