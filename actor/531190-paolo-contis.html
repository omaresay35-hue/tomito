Paolo Contis Performer | ممثل

Paolo Enrico Tusi Contis (born March 14, 1984) is a Filipino actor, host, and comedian of Italian descent. He was a popular child actor who appeared in several films during the mid-1990s. Despite being cast in the popular drama series Mara Clara, Contis is better known for being part of kids-oriented program Ang TV, and the primetime sitcom Oki Doki Doc. A GMA Network artist for 20 years, he has won several acting awards.
