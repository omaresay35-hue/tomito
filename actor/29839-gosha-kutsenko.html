Gosha Kutsenko Performer | ممثل

Yuriy Georgiyevich Kutsenko (Russian: Ю́рий Гео́ргиевич Куце́нко; born 20 May 1967; Zaporozhye), better known as Gosha Kutsenko (Russian: Гоша Куценко), is a Russian actor, producer, singer, poet, and screenwriter. Kutsenko has appeared in high-profile films such as Mama Don't Cry, Antikiller, Night Watch, Lubov-Morkov, and Echelon Conspiracy.
