Mayrín Villanueva Performer | ممثل

Mayrín Villanueva is a Mexican actress and model. She has also posed for multiple magazines. She was considered for the role of Julia Montaño in the 2004 telenovela Apuesta por un Amor; the role was eventually given to Patricia Manterola. She starred as the protagonist in the 2013 telenovela Mentir para vivir.
