Shori Kondo Performer | ممثل

Kondo Shori is a Japanese actor signed with Watanabe Entertainment and Kansai-based theater troupe Gekidan Patch. He made his acting debut on March 17, 2015 and made a name for himself in 2.5D theater. His debut drama was 2023's "Zenra Meshi," where he plays one of the male leads.
