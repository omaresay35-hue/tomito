Dito Darmawan Performer | ممثل

Bramandito Darmawan Putra (born December 24, 1999) is an Indonesian actor. Dito started his career as a director since high school, through a short film titled Réve (2016). The first short film that Dito directed won the SMAN 21 Jakarta Cinematography competition. When he entered college, Dito continued his productivity as a director through short films titled Parenthesis (2017), In Uterus (2018), Worth the Loss (2019).
