Vikrant Massey Performer | ممثل

Vikrant Massey (born April 3, 1987) is an Indian actor. He is noted for his leading roles in TV series, Dharam Veer (2008), Balika Vadhu (2009), Baba Aiso Varr Dhoondo (2010), V The Serial (2012) and Qubool Hai (2013) and in Bollywood movies Lootera and Dil Dhadakne Do. He has played the main role in the critically acclaimed 2017 Konkona Sen movie A Death in the Gunj.
