Xing Fei, also known as Fair Xing, is a Chinese actress born on October 1, 1994, in Hulunbuir, Inner Mongolia, China. She rose to fame through her breakout role in the web drama Master Devil Do Not Kiss Me and cemented her popularity with romantic hits like Put Your Head on My Shoulder and My Little Happiness. Known for her cheerful charm and relatable screen presence, Xing has starred in a variety of genres, including fantasy, youth, and modern romance dramas.
