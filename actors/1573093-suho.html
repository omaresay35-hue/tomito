Suho Performer / ممثل

Kim Jun-myeon (김준면; born May 22, 1991), known professionally as Suho (수호), is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actor under SM Entertainment. He is the leader of the South Korean boy group EXO and its sub-group EXO-K. He made his solo debut on March 30, 2020, with his first mini album Self-Portrait. Outside of his musical career, Suho has also starred in various television dramas and movies such as One Way Trip (2016), The Universe's Star (2017), Rich Man (2018), Middle School Girl A (2018), and How Are U Bread (2020).
