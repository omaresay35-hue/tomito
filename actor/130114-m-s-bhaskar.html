M. S. Bhaskar Performer | ممثل

M. S. Bhaskar (born 1950) is an Indian actor. A former theatre artiste, he has appeared in supporting roles and as a comedian in Tamil TV series and more recently in Tamil films. He is probably best known for his performances in the series Chinna Papa Periya Papa and Selvi and in the filmsSivakasi and Mozhi. In addition to acting, Bhaskar is a dubbing artiste and occasional playback singer as well.
