Jinyoung Performer | ممثل

Park Jin-young (박진영: born September 22, 1994), known professionally as Jinyoung (진영) and formerly as Jr. and Junior, is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actor under BH Entertainment. He is a member of the South Korean boy group GOT7 and the duo JJ Project. He made his solo debut on January 18, 2023 with his first mini album Chapter 0: With.
