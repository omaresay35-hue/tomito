Nazriya Nazim Performer | ممثل

Nazriya Nazim Fahadh (born on December 20, 1994) is an Indian actress and producer known for her performances primarily in the Malayalam film industry. Nazriya began her career as a child artist and swiftly transitioned into one of the most popular actresses in South Indian cinema. She made her debut as a child artist in the Malayalam film "Palunku" (2006), directed by Blessy.
