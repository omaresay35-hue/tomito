Julie de Bona Performer | ممثل

Julie de Bona (born 7 December 1980) is a French actress. Julie has Italian and Vietnamese ancestry. Her father is a computer scientist and her mother is a seamstress. She was enrolled in the faculty of biochemistry when she decided at 19 years to take a year off to try theater. She followed the course of the Montpellier Conservatory before beginning her career in the theater café and then started her first small roles in television fiction.
