Açelya Topaloğlu Performer | ممثل

Açelya Topaloğlu (born 19 November 1986) is a Turkish actress. In 2008, she made her television debut with a role in Aşk Yakar. In 2009, she briefly appeared in Arka Sokaklar and in 2012 joined the cast of Eve Düşen Yıldırım. In 2015, she was cast in her first leading role in the popular rom-com series Kaçak Gelinler, portraying the character of Almila.
