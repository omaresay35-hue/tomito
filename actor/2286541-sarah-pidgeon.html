Sarah Pidgeon Performer | ممثل

Sarah Pidgeon (born July 7, 1996) is an American actress. She has played roles in the Amazon Prime Video series The Wilds (2020–2022) and the Hulu series Tiny Beautiful Things (2023). She made her Broadway debut in the David Adjmi play Stereophonic (2024), earning her a Tony Award nomination for Best Featured Actress in a Play, and later played Stevie Ward in the slasher film I Know What You Did Last Summer (2025).
