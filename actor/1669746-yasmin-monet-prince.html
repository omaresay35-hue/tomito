Yasmin Monet Prince Performer | ممثل

Yasmin Monet Prince is an actress, known for Hanna (2019), Dark Heart (2016) and Boxing Day (2021). Prince grew up in Sydenham, South London. She joined the youth company at Shakespeare's Globe, and attended the BRIT School. Prince made her screen debut in the 2016 Mahalia Belo TV film Ellen for Channel 4.
