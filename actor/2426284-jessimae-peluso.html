Jessimae Peluso Performer | ممثل

Jessimae Peluso (born September 16, 1982) is an American stand-up comedian, podcast and TV host. She is most known from MTV’s wildly popular show, Girl Code and also as the host of Netflix’s show Tattoo Redo. She was recently made an official ambassador for Seth Rogen and Laura Rogen’s Alzheimer’s foundation, Hilarity for Charity. She also is an ambassador for the SoCal chapter of the Alzheimer’s Association. From her website bio.
