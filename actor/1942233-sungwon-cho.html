SungWon Cho Performer | ممثل

SungWon Cho, also known as ProZD, is a Korean American actor, voice actor, sketch comedian and YouTuber. He is best known for his role as Detective Joe Furuya in Anime Crimes Division and for his many voice acting roles, most notably FL4K from Borderlands 3 and Johnny from the OK K.O.! Let's Be Heroes. He is represented by Creative Artists Agency.
