The Impressionists are the most popular group in art history – millions flock every year to marvel at their masterpieces. Impressionism was born and the art world was changed forever. What led to that first groundbreaking show 150 years ago? Who were the maverick personalities that wielded their brushes in such a radical and provocative way?
