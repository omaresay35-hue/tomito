Evgeniy Tkachuk Performer | ممثل

Evgeny Valeryevich Tkachuk (Russian: Евге́ний Вале́рьевич Ткачу́к; born July 23, 1984; Ashgabat) is a Russian theater and film actor, film director, screenwriter, producer and singer. Founder and artistic director of the private Equestrian Drama theater "VelesO" in the Leningrad region (the theater was founded in 2016).
