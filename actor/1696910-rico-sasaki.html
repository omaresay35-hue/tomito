Rico Sasaki Performer | ممثل

Rico Sasaki (佐々木 李子, Sasaki Riko, November 10, 1997) is a Japanese actress, voice actress, and singer from Akita Prefecture. Her old stage names are Rico Sasaki (佐々木 莉子) and Rico. She is affiliated with Honey-Comb Entertainment and TV Asahi Music. She was a member of the vocal unit Re-connect with Kyoco from 2018 to the end of March 2019.
