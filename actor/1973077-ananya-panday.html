Ananya Panday Performer | ممثل

Ananya Panday is an Indian actress who works in Hindi films. She is the daughter of actor Chunky Panday. She made her acting debut in Student of the Year 2 in 2019, followed by a role in Pati Patni Aur Woh in the same year. These performances earned her the Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut at the 65th Filmfare Awards.
