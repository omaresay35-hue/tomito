Mitchell Slaggert Performer | ممثل

Mitchell Slaggert is an American actor and model, based in New York. He appeared in the Broad Green feature film Wish Upon, opposite Joey King and Ryan Phillippe, and directed by John Leonetti. He also appeared in the limited series El Diablo Guardian for Blim. After being scouted on the street in Wilmington N.C., he was cast as the lead of the independent film MOSS by Daniel Peddle which premiered in the US Fiction Competition at the 2017 LA Film Festival on June 21, 2017.
