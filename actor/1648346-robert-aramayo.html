Robert Aramayo Performer | ممثل

In 2021, he starred in the Netflix psychological thriller miniseries, Behind Her Eyes. On 2022 he played on The Lord of the Rings: The Rings of Power. He has won British Academy Film Award for I Swear (2025), playing John Davidson, the Scottish campaigner for Tourette syndrome.
