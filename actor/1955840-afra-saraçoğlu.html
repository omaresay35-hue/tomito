Afra Saraçoğlu Performer | ممثل

Afra Saraçoğlu (born 2 December 1997) is a Turkish actress. At the age of eighteen, her first role is in film "En Güzeli". With Özcan Deniz, Nurgül Yeşilçay, she played in "Second Chance". With Tolga Sarıtaş, she had leading role in romantic youth film "Bad Boy". With Mert Yazıcıoğlu, Kerem Bürsin, she played in "Good Game" about game players. She played in "Is This Love?" with Kubilay Aka.
