Dani Oliveros was born in Palm Beach Gardens, FL. She first joined the industry as a model in New York City after graduating from performing arts high school in Florida. She traveled throughout major U.S. cities and Europe working full time as a model before moving to Los Angeles, where she started acting. Dani started in commercials and has transitioned to pursuing acting full time, focusing on feature films.
