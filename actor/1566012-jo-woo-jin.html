Jo Woo-jin Performer | ممثل

Jo Shin-je (born January 16, 1979), known professionally as Jo Woo-jin (조우진), is a South Korean actor. He won the Best Supporting Actor award at the 40th Blue Dragon Film Awards in 2019 for his performance in the film Default (2018).
