Maaya Uchida Performer | ممثل

Maaya Uchida is a Japanese actress, singer and voice actress affiliated with the agency I'm Enterprise. She's the older sister of Yuuma Uchida, who is also a voice actor. In February 2014, she won the Best New Actress Award in the 8th Seiyu Awards.
