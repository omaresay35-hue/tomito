Arthur Chen Performer | ممثل

Chen Feiyu, also known as Arthur, is a Chinese-American actor and assistant director under TH Ent. He made his acting debut in 2010 with the drama "Sacrifice". He is known for his roles in "Secret Fruit (2017)", "Ever Night (2018)", "My Best Summer (2019)", and "Lighter & Princess (2022)". Chen Feiyu was born in the United States. His father is the renowned director Chen Kaige, and his mother is the famous actress Chen Hong. He attended Tabor Academy, a private high school in the United States.
