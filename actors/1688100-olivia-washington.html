Olivia Washington Performer / ممثل

Olivia Washington (born April 10, 1991) is an American actress. She is the daughter of actor Denzel Washington. She is known for her role as a girl with superspeed in the miniseries I’m a Virgo (2023) and also appeared in The Butler (2013), The Tragedy of Macbeth (2021) and The Little Things (2021).
