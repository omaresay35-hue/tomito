Pranav Mohanlal (born July 13, 1990) is an Indian actor who primarily works in the Malayalam film industry. Son of legendary actor Mohanlal, Pranav made his acting debut as a child artist in Onnaman (2002) and went on to win the Kerala State Film Award for Best Child Artist for his role in Punarjani (2003).
