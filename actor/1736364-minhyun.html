Hwang Min-hyun (Hangul: 황민현; born August 9, 1995), more commonly known by his stage name Minhyun, is a South Korean singer, actor, and member of the South Korean boy group NU'EST. He is also known for competing in the 2017 series Produce 101 Season 2 and earning a spot in the final lineup, project boy group Wanna One.
