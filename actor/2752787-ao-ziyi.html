Ao Ziyi Performer | ممثل

Ao Zi Yi is a Chinese actor and singer, born in Chongqing. His career started back in 2013 after he joining Times Fengjun Entertainment. He was a member of the groups Tai Feng Si Zi (Typhoon Four), Tai Feng Shi Zi (Typhoon Ten), and Tai Feng Shi Er Zi (Typhoon Twelve).
