Nirina Zubir Performer | ممثل

Nirina Raudatul Jannah Zubir (born March 12, 1980) is a two-time Piala Citra winning Indonesian actress and presenter. She is the daughter of Zubir Amin, a senior diplomat born in Pariaman, West Sumatra. Her career started as a radio DJ at MTVonSky, becoming an MTV video jockey, and subsequently leading into film roles and hosting TV shows.
