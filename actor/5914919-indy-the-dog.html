Indy the Dog Performer | ممثل

Indy the Dog is the real canine star of the 2025 American supernatural horror film "Good Boy." He is a medium-sized, intelligent, and energetic Nova Scotia Duck Tolling Retriever who plays the lead role in a supernatural thriller told almost entirely from a dog’s point of view. Indy belongs to director and co-writer Ben Leonberg, who cast his own dog in the leading role.
