Kamila Urzędowska Performer / ممثل

Kamila Urzędowska was born on the 7th of January 1994 in Nysa, where she attended Secondary School No. 2. In 2020, she graduated from PWST in Wrocław. Initially, she played minor roles, including: in the series Żmijowisko (Den of Vipers) and M jak miłość. A breakthrough in her career came with the role of Jagna in the film The Peasants from 2023, for which she was awarded the ELLE Crystal Star at the festival in Gdynia, Poland.
