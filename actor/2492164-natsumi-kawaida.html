Natsumi Kawaida Performer | ممثل

Natsumi Kawaida (川井田 夏海, Kawaida Natsumi, born February 18, 1996) is a Japanese voice actress (seiyuu) from Aichi Prefecture. She belongs to Intention K. In 2009, she appeared in the Marumiya food musical Annie as one of the child actors of the Pepper of the Tomorrow group. Her co-stars in child roles at the time also included Reika Uyama.
