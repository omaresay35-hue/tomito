Qin Junjie Performer | ممثل

Qin Junjie (Chinese: 秦俊杰, born 1 September 1991) is a Chinese actor. He made his debut in the 2006 film Curse of the Golden Flower. He is best known for his starring roles in the TV series Noble Aspirations (2016), Legend of Dragon Pearl (2017), Peace in Palace, Peace in Chang’an, and Listening Snow Tower (2019).
