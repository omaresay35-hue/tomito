Born in Havana, Cuba, Garriga began working in theatre and TV as a teenager and appeared on the show “Cuban Television Ballet.” In 2009, she relocated to Italy and studied at Michael Rodgers Acting Studio in Milan. She then studied in New York at the Terry Schreiber Studio and The Actors Studio.
