Wang Duo Performer | ممثل

Wang Duo is a Chinese actor and singer who graduated from Shenyang Conservatory of Music. In 2015, he participated in Guangdong satellite TV's "China's Good Boys" and won second place in the national finals, which attracted the attention of the media and the public. His most publicly recognized work is the character Li Si Yuan, Duke of Ning, a complex and intense character in the drama "Bloody Romance".
