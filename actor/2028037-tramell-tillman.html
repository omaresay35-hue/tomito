Tramell Tillman Performer | ممثل

Tramell Tillman (/trəˈmɛl/ trə-MEL; born June 17, 1985) is an American actor. He is known for his role as Seth Milchick in the Apple TV+ series Severance (2022–present), for which he received his first Primetime Emmy nomination for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series in 2025.
