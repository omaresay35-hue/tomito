Robert LaSardo is an American character actor and former Navy sailor. He began his career studying at the High School of Performing Arts in New York City where he became an honors student, before going on to the Stella Adler Studio of Acting. He spent four years in the U.S. Navy. For two of those years, he handled Navy attack dogs in the Aleutian Islands.
