Jiratchapong Srisang Performer | ممثل

Force Jiratchapong Srisang is a Thai actor under GMMTV. In 2019, he graduated with a bachelor's degree from the College of Communication Arts at Rangsit University, majoring in Digital Marketing. He made his acting debut in 2017 with a main role in the series "Noir" (ครั้งนั้น..ไม่ลืม). Aside his acting career, Force owns the clothing brand 'EFSEC'.
