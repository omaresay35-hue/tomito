Maris Racal Performer | ممثل

Maris Racal (born Mariestella Cañedo Racal on 22 September 1997) is a Filipino actress and singer. She is best known for her roles in the films Here Comes the Groom (2023), Where Is the Lie? (2023), And The Breadwinner Is... (2024), Sosyal Climbers (2025), and Sunshine (2025), among others.
