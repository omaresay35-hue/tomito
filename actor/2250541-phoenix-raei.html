Phoenix Raei Performer | ممثل

Phoenix Raei (born October 28, 1990) is an Australian actor, director and producer of Iranian descent. Raei, who was born in Shiraz in Iran, immigrated to Australia as a child. In 2019, he was the recipient of the Casting Guild of Australia, Rising Star Award. In 2021, he starred in the Netflix series Clickbait. Raei is engaged to Australian actress Kate Lister. The two collaborated in various previous projects, and together they manage a production company, Little Fish Films.
