Kim Jin-kwon Performer | ممثل

Kim Jin Kwon, known mononymously as Jinkwon, is a South Korean singer, dancer, actor and member of NewKidd (뉴키드). He graduated from Hanlim Entertainment Arts High School and is currently enrolled in the theater and film program at Seoil University. In 2019, he debuted as the group's leader and vocalist with their self-titled single album "Newkidd." He made his acting debut in 2018 with a role in "Love After School Season 2" (방과후 연애 2).
