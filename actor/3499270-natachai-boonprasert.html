Natachai Boonprasert Performer | ممثل

Dunk Natachai Boonprasert is a Thai actor and singer under GMMTV. In 2023, he graduated with a bachelor's degree from the School of Engineering at King Mongkut's Institute of Technology Ladkrabang, majoring in Computer Innovation Engineering. He made his acting debut in 2021 with a guest role in the series "Bad Buddy". Aside his acting and singing career, Dunk owns the clothing brand 'VESPERA' and the bakery 'NAVORI'.
