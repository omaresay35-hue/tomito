Lilly Singh Performer | ممثل

Lilly Singh is a Canadian YouTuber, comedian, talk show host and actress, formerly known as IISuperwomanII. In 2017, she was ranked tenth on the Forbes list of the world's highest paid YouTube stars, earning a reported $10.5 million. Singh has featured in the annual YouTube Rewind every year since 2014.
