Vivienne Tien Performer / ممثل

Vivienne Tien is a Chinese-American actress, born in the US and graduated from the Performance Department of Shanghai Theatre Academy. In 2017, she debuted in the sci-fi action movie "Iron Sky: The Ark." In 2021 she dubbed the character Wang Lina in the animated film "Wish Dragon".
