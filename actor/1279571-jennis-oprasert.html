Jennis Oprasert Performer | ممثل

Jennis Oprasert is a member of the Thai idol girl group BNK48, an international sister group of the Japanese idol girl group AKB48. She is one of the first-generation members of the group. She's also the vice-captain of BNK48's Team BIII and a member of BNK48's sub-unit VYRA (formerly LYRA). She began her acting career as a child actress.
