Charlotte Vega Performer | ممثل

Charlotte Elizabeth Vega (born 10 February 1994 in Madrid) is a Spanish actress and model, known for her role in the 2014 Spanish film The Misfits Club, the lead role in the 2017 Irish film The Lodgers, and main roles in two television series during 2015 – season 3 of the Spanish series Velvet and the single season of the Spanish-British co-production The Refugees. She also plays the lead role in the 2021 film Wrong Turn.
