Masashi Ikeda Performer / ممثل

Ikeda Masashi is a Japanese actor from Tokushima, Japan. He is affiliated with A-Plus. He first gained attention by reaching the Top 15 in the 31st Junon Super Boy Contest in 2018. Ikeda made his acting debut in 2021 with the television drama "Tokyo Mer: The Running Emergency Room." While attending Tokushima Prefectural Tokushima Kita High School, he represented the prefecture in the 96th National High School Soccer Championship. After moving to Tokyo for university entrance exams, he was approached by A-Plus, leading him to pursue acting and audition for special effects roles, fulfilling a childhood ambition.
