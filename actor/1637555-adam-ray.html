Adam Ray Performer | ممثل

Adam Ray is an American comedian, actor, and YouTuber. Known for his versatile comedic talent, Ray has gained recognition for his stand-up, improv, and acting work. Since 2023, he has regularly performed under the persona of "Dr. Phil," a parody of television personality Dr. Phil McGraw, blending sharp humor and impersonation. Ray's work continues to resonate with audiences across various platforms, establishing him as a dynamic presence in the entertainment industry.
