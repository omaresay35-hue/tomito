Yuri Kolokolnikov Performer | ممثل

Yuri Andreyevich Kolokolnikov (born December 15, 1980) is a Russian actor of theatre and cinema. In the West, Kolokolnikov is best known for his performance as Styr in the television series Game of Thrones and as Gennadi Bystrov in The Americans.
