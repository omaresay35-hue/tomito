Martin Jauvat Performer | ممثل

Martin Jauvat lives in Chelles (77), where he has spent his entire life. After a brief career in the world of ping-pong marked by disillusionment within the AS Chelles, he studied literature in Paris and began his collaboration with the production company Ecce films. He will direct, in his native town of Seine et Marne, Les Vacances à Chelles in 2019, Mozeb in 2020, and Le Sang de la Veine in 2021.
