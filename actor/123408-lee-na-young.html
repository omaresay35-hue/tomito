Lee Na-young Performer | ممثل

Lee Na Young is a South Korean actress, best known for her leading roles in TV Series “Ruler of Your Own World” (2002) and “Ireland” (2004). She is also known for her leading roles in “Someone Special” (2004), “Maundy Thursday” (2006), and “Romance is a Bonus Book” (2019). Aside from acting, Lee is also known for appearing in numerous commercials. Lee married actor Won Bin on May 30, 2015, in a private small ceremony in a wheat field near an inn in Won Bin's hometown, Jeongseon County, Gangwon
