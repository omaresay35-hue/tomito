Minami Hamabe Performer | ممثل

Minami Hamabe (born August 29, 2000) is a Japanese actress. She is one of Japan's most prominent figures in the modern-day entertainment industry. Hamabe gained widespread fame for her role in Japan for her role in the live-action film Let Me Eat Your Pancreas (2017). She gained more of a worldwide fame with her role and performance in Takashi Yamazaki's Godzilla Minus One (2023).
