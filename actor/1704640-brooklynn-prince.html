Brooklynn Prince Performer | ممثل

Brooklynn Kimberly Prince (born May 4, 2010) is an American actress widely known for her critically acclaimed role in the 2017 film The Florida Project, for which she won the Critics Choice Movie Award for Best Young Performer. She has since also starred in The Turning, Home Before Dark, The One and Only Ivan and Cocaine Bear.
