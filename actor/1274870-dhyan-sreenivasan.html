Dhyan Sreenivasan Performer | ممثل

Dhyan Sreenivasan is an Indian film actor known for his Malayalam films. Dhyan is the younger son of noted Malayalam screenwriter and actor Sreenivasan and Vimala. His brother Vineeth Sreenivasan is a singer, actor and director who also works in Malayalam cinema. He made his debut in 2013 with Thira, directed by his elder brother Vineeth Sreenivasan
