Tamar Novas Performer | ممثل

Tamar Novas was born on October 3, 1986 in Santiago de Compostela, A Coruña, Galicia, Spain as Tamar Novas Pita. He is an actor and editor, known for The Sea Inside (2004), Broken Embraces (2009) and Butterfly (1999).
