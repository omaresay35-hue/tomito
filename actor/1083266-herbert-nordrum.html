Herbert Nordrum (born 1987) is a Norwegian actor. He studied at the Norwegian National Academy of Theatre from 2008 to 2011. He won the Amanda Award in 2014 for Best Supporting Actor and the Kanon Award in 2013 for Best Supporting Role for his role as "Karl" in Pornopung. He had the lead role in the 2018 TV series Match.
