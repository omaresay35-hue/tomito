Ping Krittanun Aunchananun is an actor under GMMTV. He was managed by MChoice until April 30, 2025. After graduating from Bangkok Christian College, he went on to study in the Bachelor of Business Administration Program in Culinary Arts at Dusit Thani College. In 2022, Ping made his acting debut with a leading role in the series "Ai Long Nhai" (อัยย์หลงไน๋).
