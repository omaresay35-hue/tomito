Victoria Song Performer | ممثل

Victoria Song, born Song Qian on February 2, 1987, in Qingdao, Shandong, China, is a Chinese artist known for her work as a singer, dancer, actress, model, host, and author. She gained international fame as the leader of the South Korean girl group f(x), managed by SM Entertainment. Victoria's career spans music, television, and film, with roles in Chinese dramas like "When Love Walked In," "Beautiful Secret," and "Find Yourself," as well as films such as "My Best Friend's Wedding" and "Wished".
