Kavin Raj Performer | ممثل

Kavin Raj is an Indian film and television actor and television presenter. He began his career in Star Vijay as serial actor. He appeared in Kana kanum kalagal as Shiva, then played a role in Saravanan Meenatchi as Murugan and Saravanan Meenatchi part 2 as Vettaiyan . He also appeared in Intru netru nalai in a cameo appearance as heroine pair. He currently hosts a reality television show called Kings of Dance on Star Vijay. He will appear soon in Vikram Prabhu's upcoming movie Mudi Sooda Mannan
