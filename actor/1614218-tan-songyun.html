Tan Songyun Performer | ممثل

A graduate of Beijing Film Academy, she got her start as a child actor in 2005 when she filmed the TV series “Wild Chrysanthemum” (山菊花). It was in the 2005 series "Live Your Days Peacefully" (好好过日子), however, that audiences first saw her.
