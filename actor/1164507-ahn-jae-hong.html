Ahn Jae-hong Performer | ممثل

Ahn Jae-hong (안재홍) is a South Korean actor born on March 31, 1986. He is represented by J,Wide-Company. He gained recognition through his roles in the films The King of Jokgu (2014) and Fabricated City (2017) and in the television series Reply 1988 (2015–2016), Fight for My Way (2017) and Be Melodramatic (2019).
