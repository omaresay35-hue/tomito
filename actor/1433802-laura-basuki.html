Laura Basuki Performer | ممثل

Laura Basuki (born January 9, 1988) is a two-time Piala Citra winning Indonesian actress and model. Her career started with being a music video model. She debuted in Because of Soccer (2008). Her roles in 3 Hati, Dua Dunia, Satu Cinta (2010) and Susi Susanti: Love All (2019) won her the Piala Citra. She is critically acclaimed for Before, Now & Then (2022), Sleep Call (2023), and Heartbreak Motel (2024).
