Franck Dubosc Performer | ممثل

Franck Dubosc is a French comedian, actor, screenwriter, and director, born on November 7, 1963, in Le Petit-Quevilly, France. Known for his charismatic and humorous style, he gained popularity through stand-up comedy before transitioning into film and television. Dubosc's breakthrough came with the Camping film series, where he played Patrick Chirac, a role that cemented his status in French comedy. He collaborated frequently with directors like Fabien Onteniente (Disco, Camping 2, Camping 3)
