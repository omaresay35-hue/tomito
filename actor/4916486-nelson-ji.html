Nelson Ji Performer | ممثل

Nelson (Chinese: 纪淼胤) is a member of the boy band VERA from Malaysia, who gained fame on the show "ATOM BOYZ." Influenced by his father, a show singer, Nelson moved to Taiwan to follow his dreams. While studying at university, he delayed graduation to participate in "ATOM BOYZ" and has since started preparing for an acting career by taking classes and studying scripts. Nelson is making a fresh start in Taiwan with his Chinese stage name, Ji Chenghao.
