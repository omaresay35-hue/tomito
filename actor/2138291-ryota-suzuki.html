Ryota Suzuki (鈴木 崚汰, Suzuki Ryōta, December 22, 1998) is a Japanese voice actor was born in Nagoya, Aichi and raised in Chiba. He is affiliated with TMS Music. He graduated from the Nihon Narēshon Engi Kenkyū-jo (NichiNare). He was formerly affiliated with Vims agency until March 31, 2023.
