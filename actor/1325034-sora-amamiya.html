Sora Amamiya Performer | ممثل

Sora Amamiya is a Japanese voice actress and singer from Toyko, affiliated with Music Ray'n. After passing an audition held by Music Ray'n, she made her voice acting debut in 2012. She played her first main role as Kaori Fujimiya in the 2014 anime series One Week Friends.
