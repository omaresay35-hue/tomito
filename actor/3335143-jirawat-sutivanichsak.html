Jirawat Sutivanichsak Performer | ممثل

Dew Jirawat Sutivanichsak is a Thai actor and model under GMMTV. He is currently attending the College of Social Communication Innovation at Srinakharinwirot University, majoring in Interactive and Multimedia Design. Dew started in the entertainment industry as a model. He made his acting debut in 2021 with a main role in the series "F4 Thailand: Boys Over Flowers".
