Dennis Trillo Performer | ممثل

Dennis Trillo (born Abelardo Dennis Florencio Ho on 12 May 1981) is a Filipino actor. He is best known for his roles in the films Aishite Imasu 1941: Mahal Kita (2004), Felix Manalo (2016), Bakit Lahat ng Gwapo may Boyfriend? (2016), One Great Love (2018), On the Job: The Missing 8 (2021), and Green Bones (2024). He won the Best Supporting Actor Award at the 30th Metro Manila Film Festival and at the 53rd FAMAS Awards for Aishite Imasu 1941: Mahal Kita, the Best Actor award at the 64th FAMAS Awa
