Nathalie Poza Performer | ممثل

Nathalie Poza is a Spanish film, stage, and television actress. She began her career in theatre before moving into television and film in the 1990s. Poza has appeared in numerous Spanish productions, including Julieta, While at War, and Rosa’s Wedding. She is a recipient of multiple awards, including two Goya Awards.
