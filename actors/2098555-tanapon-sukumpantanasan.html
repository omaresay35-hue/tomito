Perth Tanapon Sukumpantanasan is a Thai actor and singer born in Bangkok. He graduated from Saint Dominic School and is currently a student at Srinakharinwirot University (SWU), majoring in Innovative Media Arts and Acting and Director Training. Perth made his acting debut as Breeze in the 2017 series "Please... Seiyng Reiyk Wiyyan". He rose to prominence portraying Ae in the series "Love by chance". He was part of the boy band "Tempt" which disbanded in 2020. He was one of the artists in a ten-member boy band "Boyfriends." Season 1 partnered with Ritz.
