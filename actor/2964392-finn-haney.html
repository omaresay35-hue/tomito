His passion for acting developed in high school, where he was an active participant in Tamalpais High School's Conservatory Theater Ensemble. There, he trained in various acting techniques, playwriting, technical theater, and more. He is currently studying for an acting degree at the California Institute of the Arts while continuing to audition for new role
