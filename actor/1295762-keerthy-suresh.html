Keerthy Suresh Performer | ممثل

Keerthy Suresh is an Indian film actress working in the South Indian film industries. Keerthy was a child actress in the early 2000s and, after completing her studies and pursuing a degree in fashion design, she returned to acting in lead roles. She played her first lead role in the 2013 Malayalam film Geethaanjali and has signed up films in Tamil and Telugu languages as well.
