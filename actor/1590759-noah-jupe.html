Noah Jupe Performer | ممثل

Noah Casford Jupe (born February 25, 2005) is a British actor. He is known for his roles in the television series The Night Manager (2016), the dark comedy film Suburbicon (2017), the drama film Wonder (2017); the horror film A Quiet Place (2018) and its sequel A Quiet Place Part II (2021); the sports drama film Ford v Ferrari (2019); the drama film Honey Boy (2019), for which he was nominated for the Independent Spirit Award for Best Supporting Male; and the miniseries The Undoing (2020).
