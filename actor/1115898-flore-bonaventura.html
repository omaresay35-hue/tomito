Flore Bonaventura Performer | ممثل

Flore Bonaventura is a French actress, born October 12, 1981. She discovered a passion for theater at the age of ten. From 2002 to 2005, she took drama lessons at the Marcel Dupré Conservatory in Meudon, then from 2005 to 2010, teaching at the Eva Saint-Paul drama school in Paris.
