Indra Jegel Performer | ممثل

Indra Gunawan, S.E. (born November 5, 1989), better known as Indra Jegel, is an Indonesian stand-up comedian, actor, presenter, and broadcaster. He was the winner of Stand Up Comedy Indonesia Season 6 on Kompas TV in 2016. Known for his distinctive Malay accent when performing stand-up comedy, his popularity has even reached Malaysia, where he is frequently invited to perform.
