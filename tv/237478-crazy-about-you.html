Mania de Você Crazy About You

The story begins with the ambiguous relationship between friends Viola and Luma, who seem predestined to literally enter each other's lives. Thus begins the duo's journey, which goes from a passion for recipes to an obsession with the same man, from the immediate connection to the point of starting to live their friend’s reality, 10 years later.
